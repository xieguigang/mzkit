TIC2D {GCxGC} R Documentation

Demodulate the 1D TIC to 2D data

Description

Usage

TIC2D(TIC, modtime);

Arguments

TIC

[as ChromatogramTick]

modtime

The time required to complete a cycle is called the period of modulation (modulation time) and is actually the time in between two hot pulses, which typically lasts between 2 and 10 seconds is related to the time needed for the compounds to eluted in 2D. [as double]
