SpatialMapping

Description

the spatial mapping between two spatial omics data

this mapping data is consist with a collection of the spatial SpotMap,
the data SpatialMapping.transform records how to mapping from one layer to another
omics layer, example as the geometry point rotation, offsets, and other operations.
